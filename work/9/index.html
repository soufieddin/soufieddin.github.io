Biography and portfolio site

Client: Arne Quinze Studio

Arne Quinze is a Belgian contemporary artist, painter and sculptor. His work involves everything from small drawings and paintings, medium-sized sculptures to massive installations. His early career in the 1980s was as a graffiti artist. His work quickly evolved from Street Art to Public Art with recurring themes as social interaction, urbanisation and diversity.
